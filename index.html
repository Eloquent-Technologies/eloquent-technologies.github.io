More About Us.

Eloquent's main focus is to deliver a wide range of robust, secure, fit-for-purpose and cutting-edge core IT services from our own network and server platforms that we've designed with our customers and the needs of the modern workforce in mind.

Eloquent operates a number of platforms in diverse sites in the most secure data centres in the UK, all of which are connected to our network, and from which our services are delivered. These are our own facilities and have been built with the best IT infrastructure and are all managed and maintained by our exceptionally well trained and qualified staff in order to deliver business critical services to our customers.

What we do

  • Managed Hosting - Hosted Virtual Machines, Backup, Disaster Recovery, Hosted Desktops, Physical Co-Location, Full Private Cloud & Web Hosting
  • Networks - Leased Lines, FTTC, ADSL, 4G, EFM, MPLS, Managed Routers & Web Filtering
  • Email Solutions - Office 365, Hosted Exchange, Email Filtering & Email Migrations
  • Data Backup & Disaster Recovery - Managed Backups, Cloud Backups, Monitoring, Data Replication & DRaaS (Disaster Recovery as a Service)
  • Collaboration - Dropbox for Business, Office 365
  • Support - Outsourced Service Desk, Server Support, Onsite & Call-Outs, 3rd Line, Remote Support & Monitoring
  • Professional Services - Consultancy, Systems Design, Project Management
  • Software Development - In-House Developers, Chatbots, API integrations, Hosted Version Control & Issue Management
